Panchaagni vidya - 5 processing centers

1 From swarga (surya) - 2 rain bearing Cloud forms (moon)
From cloud -  rain forms
From rain - 3 aushadhi forms (earth)
From aushadhi - 4 male seed forms
 from male seed transfers to 5 - female 

5 different transformation of jeeva in the 5 processing centers -

 jalarupa jeeva (initial form of jeeva)
1st somarupa jeeva - processing center is swarga
2nd vrishti Rupa jeeva - cloud 
3rd oshadh roopa - earth 
4th retarupa jeeva - male body 
5th manushya rupa jeeva -  seed in female body 

(Ref - Chandogya 5.3 / Brihadaranyak 6.2 / Brahma sutra 3.1 / Mundaka 2.1.4)

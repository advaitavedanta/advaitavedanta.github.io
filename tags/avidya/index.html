Nature of Ignorance / Avidya

ज्ञानेन तु तदज्ञानं येषां नाशितमात्मनः। तेषामादित्यवज्ज्ञानं प्रकाशयति तत्परम् ।।5.16।। Ignorance, or "agyaan," isn't simply the absence of knowledge but rather its adversary. It persists until displaced by knowledge. Agyaan shouldn't be misconstrued as a lack of knowledge because mere absence ("abhaava") doesn't lead to issues.
